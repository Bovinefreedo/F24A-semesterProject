Energy is the lifeblood of modern society. It powers our industries, illuminates our cities, and fuels our aspirations. Yet, for far too many, energy remains a luxury, a distant dream that seems perpetually out of reach. In our pursuit of progress, we cannot afford to leave anyone behind. That is why Goal 7 is not just about flipping a switch; it's about illuminating the path to a brighter, more inclusive future.

Did you know?

  • About 789 million people globally lack electricity access, mainly in rural areas, limiting their full participation in economic and social progress. This energy poverty deepens inequalities and hampers efforts to enhance living standards and reduce poverty.
